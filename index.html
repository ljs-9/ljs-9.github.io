About Me

关于我

I am a lecturer in finance with research interests in Financial Economics, Behavioral Finance, Asset Pricing, and Investor Sentiment. My current work focuses on volatility modeling, macro-finance interaction, the role of geopolitical risk, and the influence of investor sentiment in financial markets.

我是一名金融学讲师，研究方向包括金融经济学、行为金融学、资产定价以及投资者情绪。当前研究主要聚焦于波动率建模、宏观金融互动、地缘政治风险以及投资者情绪对金融市场的影响。
