About Me

关于我

I am a lecturer in finance with research interests in Financial Economics, Behavioral Finance, and Asset Pricing. My current work focuses on volatility modeling, macro-finance interaction, and the role of geopolitical risk in financial markets.

我是一名金融学讲师，研究方向包括金融经济学、行为金融学和资产定价。当前研究聚焦于波动率建模、宏观与金融市场的相互作用以及地缘政治风险在金融市场中的作用。
