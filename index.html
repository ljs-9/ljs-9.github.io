About Me

My research focuses on financial economics, behavioural finance, asset pricing, and volatility forecasting. I am particularly interested in how geopolitical risk, policy uncertainty, investor sentiment, and macro-financial conditions shape long-term volatility in financial markets.

Research Interests

  • Financial economics and asset pricing
  • Behavioural finance and investor sentiment
  • Volatility modelling and forecasting
  • Geopolitical risk, policy uncertainty, and macro-financial interactions
