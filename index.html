About Me

My research focuses on financial economics, behavioural finance, asset pricing, and volatility forecasting.

I am particularly interested in how geopolitical risk, policy uncertainty, investor sentiment, ESG-related uncertainty, and macro-financial conditions shape long-term volatility in financial markets.

This website presents my research profile, selected publications, curriculum vitae, teaching experience, and contact information.
