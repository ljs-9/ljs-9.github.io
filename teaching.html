Teaching

My teaching interests cover financial management, corporate finance, international finance, asset valuation, derivatives, financial data analysis, and Python-based quantitative finance.

Teaching Areas

  • Financial Management
  • Corporate Finance
  • International Finance
  • Asset Valuation
  • Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives
  • Python for Finance and Financial Data Analysis

Teaching Philosophy

I emphasise the connection between financial theory, empirical evidence, and practical decision-making. In quantitative courses, I aim to help students understand not only how to implement models, but also why the assumptions, data structure, and economic interpretation matter.
